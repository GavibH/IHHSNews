    HOSA SERVICE & FILM OPP

    The Indian Hills HOSA’s PSA team is filming a public service commercial under the 2023-2024 HOSA PSA Topic:

    Fentanyl Will Kill You.

    We plan to use our PSA to educate the community on the dangers of Fentanyl and provide helpful strategies to prevent Fentanyl use and overdose. HOSA- Future Health Professionals can make a difference!

    Our commercial consists of a cluster of students walking through a school hallway between periods. As they walk through the hallways, they each begin dropping to the floor. This is meant to represent to the audience the 150 people dying to fentanyl overdose each day. Our PSA closes with one of the bodies being zipped into a bag and carried away. We hope to involve as many students as actors, in order to display the vast number of daily fentanyl deaths.

    For the time you spend assisting us by participating as an actor you will receive a letter confirming your service, which can be applied to the NHS service requirement or other needs.

    In order to confirm your participation, we ask that you please fill out this form. Additionally, you need to come prepared with your signed media release form, which can be found here. YOU MUST have a signed media release form to participate.

    If needed, you can ask Andrew Jabaly, James Sabino, or Ms. Murphy in the 800 wing science office for a printed media release form.

    Please fill out the google form by Sunday, March 3. Filming for our PSA will begin at 3:00 on Monday, March 4 in the cafeteria and will continue until 4:00.
