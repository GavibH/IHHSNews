    JUNIORS NJGPA

    To all Juniors! The week of March 12th will be all delayed openings for everybody except Juniors (womp womp). The Juniors will come into school at a 7:35 and go to their designated rooms for testing. The test is the New Jersey Student Learning Standards (NJSLS) for Grade 10 English Language Arts and Algebra 1 and Geometry for Mathematics. Juniors must bring:

  • 1) No. 2 pencils with erasers. 2) Wired earphones with a compatible jack for the student’s laptop. Headphones are required for the English units. Bluetooth headphones are not compatible with the testing software. 3) An approved graphing calculator. Students who do not have a graphing calculator can use the online calculator provided through the testing environment
